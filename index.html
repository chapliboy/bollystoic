Bollywood Stoicism

Finding Stoic Wisdom in Bollywood Lyrics

Stoicism is an ancient philosophy, and one of its core teachings is that there is a difference between the things that we can control, and the things that we cannot. I believe that wisdom that rings true can be found in all places, so here, we look for stoic wisdom in the lyrics of various bollywood songs.

Ghatni hi thi yeh bhi ghatna, ghathe ghathe lo ye ghat gayi

This occurence always had to occur, and as other occurrences occurred, this too occurred.

Song: Chor Bazaari

Film: Love Aaj Kal

Darna kya, bhala bura, hone do ab jo bhi ho

What is the need to fear the desirable or the undesirable. Whatever is meant to happen, let it happen.

Song: Pehli Baar

Film: Dil Dhadakne Do
